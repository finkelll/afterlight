Family Organizer

Create Room & Share

Create a room for someone who has died, add Moments and/or Tributes, copy the room URL, and share so others can visit and contribute.
1

Land on product

Search, word of mouth, or direct link. Organizer is likely tired and hoping something will be simple.

2

Create account

Email + name. Lightweight — no social login required.

Required
3

Create room

Main image of the person (hero photo). Optional: first/last name, dates (year only, month+year, or full date), places of birth and death, additional photos.

All fields optional except a way to identify the room
4

Add Moments & Tributes

Choose from starter suggestions (e.g. "Her laugh," "Stories we tell," "A song for her") or create custom ones. The Moment/Tribute distinction helps organizers set up meaningful structure.

Starter suggestions available
5

Set visibility & approval

Public (discoverable) or link-only. Optionally require approval for new candles before they appear.

Link-only (default): Only people with the URL can find and open the room. URL uses a random slug — not guessable.
Public: Room is discoverable/listable. Room title and description are indexable.
Approval on: New candles go to a pending queue; organizer approves before they appear.
6

Copy link & share

The room URL is the shareable link — no separate invite system. Room settings include "Copy link" and "Copy message" (pre-written text with the URL and a warm line).

Copy link · Copy message
7

See candles from others

First non-creator candle = "aha moment." The space is working as a shared home.

✓ Organizer prompted once: "How is the space feeling so far?" (optional qualitative check-in)
8

Re-share when needed

Same URL. Use "Copy link" or "Copy message" anytime — e.g. around anniversaries, when thinking of the person.

Contributor

Visit & Light a Candle

Open the room via URL, see who it's for, read candles, and add a candle (text + optional photo or link) with minimal friction. No account required.
1

Open room URL

Tap link from email, chat, or bookmark. No account required to view.

2

Room view

See who the room is for: main image, optional name/dates/places, and list of Moments and Tributes with candle counts.

3

Open a Moment or Tribute

Tap to enter. Contributor doesn't need to worry about the Moment/Tribute distinction — "share whatever feels right."

Low-stakes choice
4

Read candles (spread)

Each candle shown individually: name (or "Someone"), message, optional photo or link, "Lit X ago." No aggregation.

4b

Empty state (if no candles yet)

"No one has shared here yet. It can feel hard to go first — but even a few words, or just your name, is a way of saying 'I was here, and I remember.' Whatever you share is enough."

Acknowledges the courage of going first
5

Tap "Light a candle"

Hero CTA — one per view. Available from room and from within each Moment/Tribute.

Primary CTA · "Light" or "Light a candle"
6

In-page form expands

Form expands inline within the Moment/Tribute view (never a modal). Message textarea with reassurance copy visible. Name or "Someone." Optional "Add photo" and link field.

Message required Photo, link, name optional
7

Submit

Button: "Light candle." Disabled state while submitting. Photo upload shows progress.

✗ Missing message → inline validation, no blame. Submit failure → clear message and retry.
8

Confirmation & candle in spread

"Thank you. Your candle is now part of this [moment/tribute]." Candle appears in the list. If approval is on: "Your candle will appear once the room creator approves it."

✓ Optional pulse: "Did this feel right?" (single-question, non-intrusive, never repeated in session)
Family Organizer

Moderate (Remove Candle)

Remove an inappropriate or mistaken candle and keep the room safe — without contacting support.
1

Open room as creator

Navigate to the Moment/Tribute where the candle appears.

2

See problematic candle

"Remove" / "Delete candle" is visible to room creator only. Unobtrusive — doesn't dominate the view.

3

Confirm removal

Small confirmation dialog (not the Light form pattern — this is a destructive action). Clear Confirm / Cancel.

✗ If removal fails, show clear message and retry.
4

Candle removed

Candle disappears from the Moment/Tribute. Feels like restoring safety, not punitive.

5

Optional: re-share room link

Organizer can re-share with a note ("Link still works; please only share with people who knew [name]").

Contributor or Organizer

Return Visit

Come back to the room — on an anniversary, when thinking of the person, or whenever. Same URL, no re-onboarding.
1

Open same URL

Bookmark, saved link, or re-shared by organizer. Same room, same URL — no changes.

2

Room view (unchanged)

Familiar room. May see new candles since last visit. Optional gentle cue: "This space is still here."

No re-onboarding, no clutter
3

Browse or light a new candle

Read Moments, Tributes, and candles. Light a new candle if moved to. No forced next steps.

Edge case: Lost link

Contributor lost the URL

If the room is link-only, search won't find it. The contributor asks the organizer or family for the URL again. Once they have it → same room view as before.
